Quantity handles how many rectangles will be generated. Number of cycles determinates how many rectangle will compose the final image. The following settings will be applied for each cycle. Starting R is the amount of rectangles that will be generated for the first generation N of generations is the amount of generations after the first one Top selections tells how many rectangles will be selected from the past generation and used to make the new one New R is the amount of rectangles that will be generated for each rectangle of top selection. The difference between generations can be set in 'New gen diff'
